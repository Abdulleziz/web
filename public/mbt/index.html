Active Main Battle Tanks In Service

M1 Abrams USA

M1 Abrams is a third generation main battle tank designed by Chrysler Defence.

T-90 Russia

The T-90 is a third-generation Russian main battle tank developed to replace the T-72.

Leopard 2 Germany

The Leopard 2 is a third generation German main battle tank developed by Krauss-Maffei in the 1970s.

T-80B Russia

The T-80 is a main battle tank that was designed and manufactured in the Soviet Union.

Challenger 2 United Kingdom

The FV4034 Challenger 2 (MoD designation "CR2") is a third generation British main battle tank.

T-72 Russia

The T-72 is a family of Soviet main battle tanks that entered production in 1969.

Leclerc France

The Leclerc is a third-generation French main battle tank developed and manufactured by Nexter Systems.

ZTZ-99 China

The Type 99 (Chinese: Jiǔjiǔshì Zhǔzhàn Tǎnkè) or ZTZ-99 is a Chinese third generation main battle tank.

VT-4 (MBT-3000) China

The VT-4 (Chinese: VT-4 zhǔzhàn tǎnkè) is a Chinese third generation mbt built by Norinco for overseas export.

K2 Black Panther South Korea

K2 Black Panther (Korean: K-2 흑표) is a South Korean main battle tank, designed by the Agency for Defense Development.

M60T Sabra Turkey

The Sabra is an extensively upgraded M60 tank developed by Israel.The Sabra is known as the M60T in Turkish service.

Merkava Israel

The Merkava (Hebrew: מרכבה) is a main battle tanks used by the Israel Defense Forces.

TKX (Type 10) Japan

The Type 10 (10式戦車, Hitomaru-shiki sensha) is a fourth generation main battle tank of JSDF.

C1 Ariete Italy

The C1 Ariete (English: battering ram) is a 3rd generation main battle tank of the Italian Army.

Karrar Iran

The Karrar (Persian كرار Striker or "Attacker") is an Iranian MBT that was announced in 2016.

Altay Turkey

Altay is a Turkish fourth generation MBT designed by Otokar based on Korean K2 to be produced by BMC.

T-14 Armata Russia

The T-14 Armata (Russian: Т-14 Армата) is a Russian MBT based on the Armata Universal Combat Platform.

Stridsvagn 122 Sweden

Stridsvagn 122 is a Swedish MBT that like the German Leopard 2A5, is based on the German Leopard 2.
